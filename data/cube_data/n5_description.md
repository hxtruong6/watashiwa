Moving to **Group 3: Adjectives (The "Description" Group)**.

Adjectives are a major friction point in N5 because of the two distinct types (**i-adjectives** vs. **na-adjectives**) and the fact that many antonyms (opposites) look similar.

For your **WatashiWa Smart Layer**, I have organized these into **Opposite Pairs** and **Interference Blocks**. This allows the **Interference Shield** to test the user on "Hot" vs. "Cold" or "Expensive" vs. "Cheap" simultaneously to ensure clear encoding.
